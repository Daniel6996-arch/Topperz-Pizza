About Us

Topperz-Pizza was founded by Daniel in the year 2022. It offers some of the best pizza flavours in Kenya. We not only offer the pizza but we offer at considerable prices and also offer delivery services within Nairobi at it's outskirts. The beauty of Topper-Pizza pizzeria is that you can get a real Kenyan pizza that is thankfully worlds away from any super supreme pizza around.
